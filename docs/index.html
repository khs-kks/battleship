How to play Battleships

Rules

The objective of Battleships is to try and sink all of the opponent's ships before they sink all of yours. Players take turns firing shots to attempt to hit the enemy ships.

Both players can not see the fleet of the other, you have to guess where is the ideal position to shoot at!

First player to sink all the opponent's ships wins the game!

Controls

Before starting, you need to place all your ships on the board. To do that, you can:

  • Choose the starting coordinates (row and column ) for each of your ships
  • Choose if you want vertical or horizontal placement
  • You can hover over the grid to check for exact coordinates

Once that is done, press start and try to defeat the AI!
